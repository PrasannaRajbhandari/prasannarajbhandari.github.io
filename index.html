Image Processing of Big Data for Plant Diseases of Four Different Plant Categories: Represented by Rice, Banana, Sunflower, and Potato

Authors: Richard S. Segall, Prasanna Rajbhandari

International Journal of Computer Vision and Image Processing (IJCVIP), IGI Global, 2024

ðŸ”— View Full Article

This research presents an automated approach for early detection of plant diseases using image processing. By analyzing the color features of leaf areas and applying k-means and GLCM, it classifies diseases in rice, banana, sunflower, and potato plants. A six-stage pipeline is used for detecting diseases like leaf smut, black sigatoka, leaf scars, and late blight.
